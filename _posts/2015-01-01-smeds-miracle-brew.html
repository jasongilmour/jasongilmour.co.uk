I developed the existing brand and web presence into a cohesive brand that would cover their Boutique and Collection and Showroom (wholesale) sub-brands, each of which is adapted to be more suitable for their varied target audiences. date: 2015-01-01 00:06:00 categories: projects projecttype: "Personal project" ---

I wanted to enhance some home made liquors by adding a novelty aesthetic and some more fun their bottling process. The label was inspired by Big Audio Dynamite’s hit 1980’s song, Medicine Show and given a fitting aesthetic and some suitable adapted lyrics.
